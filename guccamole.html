GUCCAMOLE

"Smash it, scoop it, love it — Guacamole goodness!"

Serves: 4–6 people

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Rest Time: Optional (15–30 minutes for better flavor)

Cooking Time: None (No cooking needed!)

Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons chopped cilantro
  • 2 roma tomatoes, diced
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cut the avocados in half, remove the seed, and scoop out the flesh into a bowl.
  2. Mash the avocado with a fork.
  3. Add lime juice and salt. Mix well.
  4. Add onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic. Stir gently.
  5. Add a pinch of cayenne pepper if you like a little spice.
  6. Serve immediately or refrigerate for later.

FAQs:

Q: Can I make guacamole ahead of time?
A: Yes! To keep it fresh, store it in an airtight container with a layer of plastic wrap directly on the surface to prevent browning.

Q: What can I use instead of lime?
A: If you don’t have lime, you can use lemon juice for a similar tangy flavor.

Q: How long does guacamole last?
A: Guacamole is best eaten fresh but can be stored in the fridge for up to 2 days.
