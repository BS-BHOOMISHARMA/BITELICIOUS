GUCCAMOLE

"Smash it, scoop it, love it — Guacamole goodness!"

Serves: 4–6 people

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Rest Time: Optional (15–30 minutes for better flavor)

Cooking Time: None (No cooking needed!)

Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons chopped cilantro
  • 2 roma tomatoes, diced
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (optional)
