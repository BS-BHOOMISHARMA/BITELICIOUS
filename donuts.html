Classic Donuts

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/4 tsp (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup warm water (110°F/45°C)
  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 egg
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • Oil for frying
  • Powdered sugar or glaze (optional)
