DONUTS

“Timeless Taste: The Perfect Classic Donuts Recipe”

Serves: 10–12 donuts

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Rest Time: 1 hour

Cooking Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/4 tsp (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup warm water (110°F/45°C)
  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 egg
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • Oil for frying
  • Powdered sugar or glaze (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Dissolve yeast in warm water. Let sit 5–10 minutes until foamy.
  2. Mix milk, sugar, salt, butter, and egg. Add yeast mixture.
  3. Add flour gradually to form dough. Knead 5–7 minutes.
  4. Cover and rise for 1 hour until doubled.
  5. Roll out dough, cut donuts, and let rise 30 more minutes.
  6. Fry at 350°F (175°C) for 1–2 mins per side.
  7. Drain and coat with sugar or glaze.
  8. Serve warm and enjoy!

FAQs:

Q: Can I bake the donuts instead of frying?
A: Yes! Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 10–12 minutes.

Q: How do I store leftover donuts?
A: Airtight container for 1–2 days. Reheat before eating.

Q: Can I freeze the dough?
A: Yes! Freeze after shaping. Thaw and rise before frying.
