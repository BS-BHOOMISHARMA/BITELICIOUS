Classic Donuts

Serves: 10–12 donuts

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Rest Time: 1 hour

Cooking Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/4 tsp (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup warm water (110°F/45°C)
  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 egg
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • Oil for frying
  • Powdered sugar or glaze (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve yeast in warm water. Let sit for 5–10 minutes until foamy.
  2. In a large bowl, combine milk, sugar, salt, butter, and egg. Add the yeast mixture and stir well.
  3. Add flour gradually, mixing until a soft dough forms. Knead for 5–7 minutes until smooth.
  4. Cover and let dough rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  5. Roll dough out on a floured surface to about 1/2 inch thickness. Cut out donut shapes.
  6. Let the shaped donuts rest for another 30 minutes to rise again slightly.
  7. Heat oil in a deep pan to 350°F (175°C). Fry donuts 1–2 minutes per side until golden brown.
  8. Drain on paper towels and coat with powdered sugar or glaze if desired.
  9. Serve warm and enjoy!
