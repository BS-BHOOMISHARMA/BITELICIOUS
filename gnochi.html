Instructions:

  4. Knead gently to form a soft, cohesive dough. Avoid overworking.

FAQs:

Q: Can I freeze the gnocchi?
A: Yes! Freeze on a tray, then store in a bag. Boil straight from frozen.

Q: Do I need to use russet potatoes?
A: Yes, for the fluffiest texture. Waxy potatoes make the dough sticky.

Q: Can I make this gluten-free?
A: Use a 1:1 gluten-free flour blend. Results may vary slightly.
