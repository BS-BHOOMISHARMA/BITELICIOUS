GNOCCHI

“Pillow-Soft Perfection: Homemade Potato Gnocchi Recipe”

Serves: 4–6

Prep Time: 30 minutes

Rest Time: 15 minutes

Cooking Time: 5–7 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds (900g) russet potatoes
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour (plus more as needed)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Optional: pinch of nutmeg or grated plant-based Parmesan

Instructions:

  1. Boil whole potatoes until tender, about 20 minutes. Let cool slightly.
  2. Peel and mash the potatoes while still warm.
  3. Spread potatoes on a work surface, sprinkle with flour and salt, and drizzle with olive oil.
  4. Knead gently to form a soft, cohesive dough. Avoid overworking.
  5. Divide dough into ropes, cut into 1-inch pieces, and shape with a fork if desired.
  6. Let gnocchi rest 15 minutes before cooking.
  7. Boil in salted water until they float (2–3 minutes). Remove with a slotted spoon.
  8. Serve with your favorite sauce – tomato, pesto, or butter sage (vegan if desired).

FAQs:

Q: Can I freeze the gnocchi?
A: Yes! Freeze on a tray, then store in a bag. Boil straight from frozen.

Q: Do I need to use russet potatoes?
A: Yes, for the fluffiest texture. Waxy potatoes make the dough sticky.

Q: Can I make this gluten-free?
A: Use a 1:1 gluten-free flour blend. Results may vary slightly.
