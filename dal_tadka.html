Dal Tadka

Serves: 2-3 people

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Cooking Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup toor dal (pigeon peas)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 medium onion (chopped)
  • 1 medium tomato (chopped)
  • 2-3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 tsp ginger (grated)
  • 1-2 green chilies (slit)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • A pinch of hing (asafoetida)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves (chopped)

Instructions:

  1. Rinse toor dal thoroughly. Pressure cook with water, turmeric, and a pinch of salt for 3-4 whistles until soft.
  2. Heat ghee or oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds, mustard seeds, and let them splutter.
  3. Add garlic, ginger, red chilies, and hing. Saut√© for a few seconds.
  4. Add chopped onions and green chilies. Cook until onions turn golden.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  6. Mix in the cooked dal. Adjust salt and add water for desired consistency.
  7. Simmer for a few minutes. Garnish with coriander leaves.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice or roti.
